CROISSANTS

Ingredients needed:

  • All purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Dry yeast
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Unsalted butter

Recepie

  1. Prepare the Dough
  2. Prepare the Butter Block
  3. Laminate the Dough
  4. Shape the Croissants
  5. ake Brush with egg wash (1 beaten egg). Bake at 190°C (375°F) for 20 minutes until golden brown.
